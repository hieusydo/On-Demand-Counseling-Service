ASC

Alexa Service for Counseling

ASC an end-to-end Alexa counseling application that helps mitigate the long waiting issue with psychiatry appointments by providing a platform that connects patients directly with counselors. Users of our application are college students and counselors. When the patients use our application, they are first directed to talk to a bot and describe their issue. The bot acts as the primary caregiver and tries to achieve the following goals: * Ask leading questions to help the user describe the problem * Categorize the issue according to some specified categories * Provide some simple resources to help the patient
