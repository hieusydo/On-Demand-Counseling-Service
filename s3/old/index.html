Help!

Finding available health services can be an exhausting process, especially when it comes to therapy appointments. A research from the American Psychiatric Association found that the average wait time was as long as 25 days for the first visit. We believe that it should not take such a long time to get connected with a physician. Therefore, we would like to provide a solution.
